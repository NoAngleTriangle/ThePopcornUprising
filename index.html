The Food Empire of Scratch

        
        
Overview



Flag of the FEOS

        
The Food Empire of Scratch is one of the largest alliances in Scratch Politics consisting of nations that are food-themed.
It was founded by @NickNackTheTitaniac
 

        
History

The idea of a "Food Empire of Scratch" was first mentioned on May 19, 2024 by @NickNackTheTitaniac, as seen in the image below:
        


However, it wasn't until October 3rd, 2024 that the FEOS was actually created. 


        
Member Nations

The Food Empire of SCratch currently has 3 member nations, ranked by followers.

1. The Great Egg Army
2. The Apple Empire
3. The Popcorn Uprising
